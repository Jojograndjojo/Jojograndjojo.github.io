Makers Academy Graduate

18 August 2016

So I just graduated from MA. This is the point where I am meant to find a job. The course and living in London for 3 months with no income is obviously expensive. It would be tempting to jump at the first opportunity that presents itself right?

Well yes, but that will not be the course of action I decided to take, the truth is right now, I have very little idea about what kind of jobs are out there, even though there seem to be a lot of jobs available.

What I am planning to do over the next few weeks is meet as many professional as I can, not in the hope of finding a job (although if something cool presents itself, why not...), but to find out how different professionals are actually working what are they working on and see if it would something I would see myself doing.

I happened to go to the an agile Meetup in about an hour, which should be interesting as it is the way I would like to be working, I need to remember to ask the following questions :

  • What kind of work do you do?
  • What does a week of work looks like?
  • How many people are in your team?
  • Do you use TDD ?
  • Do you have retros?
  • Do you use pair programming?
  • What kind of tasks your juniors do?

I actually need to leave now if I don't want to be late, I shall edit that post in the future and update the questions if needed...

Jonathan Passe
