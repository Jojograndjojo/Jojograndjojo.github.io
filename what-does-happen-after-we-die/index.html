What does happen after we die?

14 June 2016

I won't pretend that I have an answer to that, however if it was up to me, we would get the opportunity to watch a film of our lives.
Seeing ourselves learning to take a first step, then a second one, and another one, to fall, to stand up again, take another step...
Actually thinking about it, that movie would be long (I hope, very long and happy in my case) and probably very boring, I mean it already took me ages to describe myself learning how to walk.
But I guess that being the afterlife and all, Ingmar Bergman, or Federico Fellini could edit the movie of my life, to only show the interesting parts.
I like this idea but in case it doesn't happen, I will use this platform to tell the tale of my journey on the path to become a developer, which hopefully will bear some interest ( and maybe one will be adapted into a movie... you know in the afterlife)

Jonathan Passe
